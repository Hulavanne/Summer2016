Notes about Scenery Drawing Order:

Higher Numbers are drawn last (therefore appear after others)
Lower Numbers are drawn first (therefore appear before others)

How Scenery is sorted:

   Layer Order :

> Layer 10: Backgrounds
> Layer 9: Objects
> Layer 8: Enemies
> Layer 7: NPCs
> Layer 6: Player
> Layer 5: Foregrounds
> Layer 4: (empty)
> Layer 3: (empty)
> Layer 2: (empty)
> Layer 1: (empty)
